What is nanoARC?

nanoARC is a 1.5x1.5cm robot controller that is controllable over Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE 4.0). It is capable of independently driving two DC motors and a servo/ESC - perfect for small robots. Additional features, such as extra motor drivers or motion sensors, can be added by using expansion boards.

Firmware

The firmware that runs on the TI CC2640 microcontroller is available in this repository. TI-RTOS, TI's BLE stack and CCS is used for development.
